About Me!

Hello! I'm a third year undergraduate at UC Berkeley from Imperial Beach, a small town in San Diego right next to the border. I'm Filipino but grew up around a lot of Mexican culture; I really love Mexican food and the Spanish language. I'm a huge GoT fan, I'm always listening to music (favorite artist right now is Miguel), and I love to cook. Besides that I like to make playlists, explore cities, pretend I can sing, chill at places with beautiful views, connect with people, and try new things!

I'm a first generation college student that comes from an under-resourced community, and all the struggle that came with that has instilled within me an intense passion to help others. I believe that computer science and tech in general has so much power, and it's important that we use that power for good. I'm always thinking of projects that could help other people and all the ways in which technology can solve the world's biggest problems.
